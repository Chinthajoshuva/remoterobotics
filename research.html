Research Areas

Explore our cutting-edge research in Unmanned Ground Vehicles (UGVs) and Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs)

Unmanned Ground Vehicles (UGVs)

Autonomous Navigation

Developing systems that allow UGVs to navigate and interact with their environment without human intervention.

Robot Perception & Sensing

Using various sensors (LiDAR, cameras, etc.) to help UGVs perceive and understand their surroundings.

Path Planning & Decision Making

Creating algorithms for real-time decision-making and optimal path planning in dynamic environments.

Human-Robot Interaction

Designing intuitive interfaces and collaboration strategies between humans and ground robots.

Multi-Robot Systems

Research on coordinating multiple UGVs for collaborative tasks like exploration and surveillance.

Robotic Manipulation

Enabling UGVs to perform tasks such as object handling and construction in unstructured environments.

Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs)

Autonomous Flight Control

Developing algorithms for autonomous flight, including path planning and obstacle avoidance.

Swarming & Multi-Agent Systems

Exploring coordinated missions with multiple UAVs for surveillance and monitoring.

AI & Machine Learning

Using ML models for real-time decision-making and vision-based navigation.

Drone Design & Optimization

Focusing on lightweight materials and efficient propulsion systems for better performance.

Environmental Applications

Developing UAVs for environmental monitoring and agriculture applications.

Delivery & Logistics

Researching cargo delivery and logistics applications for UAVs.
